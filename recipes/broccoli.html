Roasted Broccoli

Description

Tender, flavorful broccoli roasted fresh in the oven

Healthy, tasty, and a perfect side for any meal

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon Montreal steak seasoning
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil

Directions:

  1. Preheat an oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C)
  2. ombine the broccoli and bell pepper in a bowl. Sprinkle the steak seasoning, chili powder, garlic powder, salt, and pepper over the vegetables; drizzle with the olive oil and toss to coat. S pread the vegetables into a shallow baking dish
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until the vegetables are tender and beginning to brown, 15 to 20 minutes.
